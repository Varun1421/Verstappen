Dominance Redefined

Max Verstappen's 2023 Season

Varun Brahma

It is easy to assume Verstappen’s dominance came purely from the Red Bull car. But his teammate, Sergio Pérez, drove the same machinery throughout the season. These small multiples compare both drivers across the same performance metrics to show that the difference was not just the car, but also consistency, execution, and the ability to convert opportunities into wins, podiums, and points.

Small Multiples: Same Car, Different Results

Each row represents one driver, while each column tracks a different performance metric across the season. Because every panel uses the same race progression on the x-axis and a consistent scale within each metric, the visual comparison is direct and easy to scan.
